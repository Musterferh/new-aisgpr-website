Who We Are

From a Grassroots Response to a Regional Movement

Climate Warden began in Bauchi State as a grassroots response to the visible environmental challenges facing Bauchi State, Nigeria. Recognizing that the youth are the most significant stakeholders in our climate future, we launched this initiative to bridge the gap between climate awareness and community action.

What started as a local movement has evolved into a structured organization dedicated to fostering a generation of environmentally conscious leaders — young people who don't just understand the climate crisis, but are actively equipped to solve it.

Today, Climate Warden is a growing force across Bauchi State, Nigeria, mobilising students, schools and communities toward a resilient and sustainable future — one warden at a time.

Mission & Vision

The principles guiding every decision, program and partnership we undertake.

Our Mission

To empower the next generation of Bauchi State, Nigerian youth through comprehensive climate education, leadership development, and sustainable community-led environmental action.

Our Vision

To build a resilient and sustainable Bauchi State, Nigeria where every student and community member is an active warden of their environment.

Our Core Values
