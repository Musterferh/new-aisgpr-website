FAQs

Common Questions

Quick answers to what we're asked most often.

Who can participate in your leadership training?

Our strategic leadership programs are designed for emerging leaders, public sector officials, civil society actors, and early-career policymakers across Africa.

How can we partner with the Institute for research?

We actively collaborate with academic institutions, government agencies, and international development organizations. Please reach out via our contact form to discuss joint policy research initiatives.

Where is your current operational focus?

While headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria, our research and policy advocacy span across the African continent, with a strong focus on regional governance frameworks.

Are your policy briefs publicly available?

Yes. We strongly believe in open access to knowledge. All of our published impact reports, policy briefs, and guides can be downloaded for free from our Publications page.

How is the Institute funded?

We are a registered NGO funded through strategic grants, international donor partnerships, and contributions from philanthropic organizations aligned with our governance mission.

How quickly do you respond to partnership inquiries?

Our dedicated team aims to respond to formal partnership and media inquiries within 48 hours on business days.
